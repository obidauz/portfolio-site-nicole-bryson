About

My Story

I am a budding UX designer who just completed the UX Design Immersion course at CareerFoundry. I love learning and am constantly taking classes about things I find interesting, which is evident in the multiple degrees I have pursued. I have a BA in film studies with a minor in digital art in computer science. My minor allowed me to explore game design, 3D graphics, graphic design, and building interactive art pieces. I also have a BS in veterinary technology which became my profession for the last 9 years. While I love helping animals, I really missed the creativity in digital art and sought out a career change where a few of my skills may translate, and thus, stumbled upon UX Design.

I spent almost a decade being a user experience designer for animals. I would make sure their desires were met constantly, from figuring out what type of food a finicky cat might want to eat, to setting up their cage in such a way that they were happy, comfortable, and felt safe. Most importantly, I was an advocate for those without a voice, which is a skill I plan to use to advocate insistently for my users.

I now have a certificate in UX Design through CareerFoundry where I learned how to create user personas and user flows, build wireframes and prototypes, conduct user research, and construct user-centered responsive designs. I also completed the UI for UX Designers specialty course. There I was able to delve deeper into UI/UX design by designing for different breakpoints, using visual design principles, applying color and image theory, and creating style guides for handoff and collaboration.

I would love to work as a UX/UI designer for a company that believes in leaving the world better than they found it, and if they want to help animals, that is just a plus!
